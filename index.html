Common Questions

I'm overwhelmed. Which series should I start with?

Start with your biggest pain point right now. Struggling with your eyes every day? Go with Shmeiras Einayim. Marriage feeling stuck? Jump into Shalom Bayis. Dating going nowhere? That series will help. The best series is the one that addresses what's actually bothering you today, not someday.

How much time commitment are we talking about?

Episodes run 15-30 minutes - perfect for your commute or lunch break. New episodes come out regularly, but here's the thing: everything's archived. You can binge a whole series or take it slow. Work at your pace. This is about real change, not checking boxes.

I'm not that learned. Will I understand these?

If you can understand this website, you can understand the podcasts. I explain every Hebrew term, break down concepts simply, and focus on application over theory. Whether you learned in yeshiva for years or just started getting serious about Judaism, you'll get practical tools you can use immediately.

Are these only for Orthodox Jews?

The content comes from Orthodox sources, absolutely. But the wisdom applies to anyone seeking authentic Jewish guidance. Relationship advice works whether you wear a black hat or not. Speech ethics matter in every community. If you want real Torah wisdom - not watered down, but accessible - you're in the right place.

Do I need to listen in order?

Depends on the series. Mesilas Yesharim and Shabbos Malkesa build step by step - definitely start at the beginning. For Dating and Shalom Bayis, you can jump to topics that matter most right now, though the full series gives you the complete picture. When in doubt, start at episode one.

What's the difference between the Hebrew and English marriage series?

The Hebrew series speaks directly to Israeli and Hebrew-speaking cultural contexts - different family dynamics, community expectations, and communication styles. The English series addresses couples in international Jewish communities. Same Torah principles, different cultural application. Choose the one that fits your reality.
