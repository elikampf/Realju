Find Your Bashert with Torah Wisdom

Navigate the world of dating and engagement through the lens of Torah. This series offers practical guidance, spiritual insights, and real-world advice for Jewish singles seeking their bashert.

From building yourself before finding someone else to making it through engagement successfully, Rabbi Klapper provides the tools you need for this crucial life journey.

Whether you're just starting to date or preparing for marriage, this comprehensive series addresses the real challenges modern Jewish singles face with authentic Torah wisdom.
